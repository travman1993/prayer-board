Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or phone) when you visit a website. They can store information about your preferences, login status, or browsing activity.

2. How Lifted Uses Cookies

Minimal Cookie Usage

Lifted uses very few cookies. Here's what we store:

Local Storage (Not Traditional Cookies)

Instead of cookies, Lifted primarily uses browser localStorage to remember:

  • Onboarding Status: Whether you've seen our community guidelines (so we don't show it every visit)
  • Posts: Your posts and confessions (all stored on YOUR device)

Third-Party Ads

If Lifted displays advertisements (e.g., Google AdSense), those ad networks may use their own cookies for:

  • Tracking ad performance
  • Personalizing ads
  • Frequency capping (not showing the same ad too often)

We recommend checking the privacy policies of our ad partners for details.

3. Cookie Choices

Disable Cookies

You can disable cookies in your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
  • Firefox: Preferences → Privacy & Security → Cookies
  • Safari: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
  • Edge: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

Clear Cookies & LocalStorage

You can clear all stored data including localStorage by:

  • Chrome: Settings → Privacy and Security → Clear browsing data
  • Firefox: Settings → Privacy & Security → Clear Data
  • Safari: Develop → Empty Web Storage (or Preferences → Privacy)

Note: Clearing your data will delete any posts or preferences you've saved on Lifted.

4. LocalStorage vs Cookies

Lifted primarily uses localStorage instead of cookies because:

  • It's more secure for storing sensitive information
  • Users have clear control over it
  • It's not sent to servers automatically
  • It respects privacy better

5. Do Not Track (DNT)

If you have "Do Not Track" enabled in your browser, Lifted respects that preference. We do not use tracking technology to follow you across the web.

6. Analytics

Lifted does not currently use analytics tools that track individual users. We may use aggregated, anonymized traffic data to understand how the site is used, but this does not identify you personally.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about how we use cookies or localStorage, please contact us.

9. Summary

The bottom line: Lifted respects your privacy. We use minimal cookies, primarily relying on localStorage to store your own posts on YOUR device. We do not track you across the web or sell your data.
