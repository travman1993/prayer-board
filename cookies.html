Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device. Lifted uses minimal storage and only for essential purposes.

What Storage We Use

Storage Item Purpose Duration
lifted-onboarded Remembers if you've seen our onboarding message Until you clear browser data

This is stored only on your device and never sent to our servers. It doesn't track you or collect any personal information.

Third-Party Services

Lifted does not use third-party tracking, analytics, or advertising services.

How to Control Storage

Most web browsers allow you to control local storage through settings. You can clear browser data at any time.

Note About Functionality

If you clear browser data, you'll need to acknowledge the onboarding message again when you visit.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date shows when this policy was last revised.

Contact

If you have questions about our use of storage, please contact us.
