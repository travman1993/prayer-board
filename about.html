About Lifted

Lifted is an anonymous community prayer and confession board designed to provide a safe, judgment-free space where people can share what's on their heart and receive encouragement from others who believe in the power of prayer and faith.

Our Mission

We believe that vulnerability, when met with compassion, creates powerful connections. Our mission is to foster a community where:

  • People feel safe sharing their deepest struggles
  • No one is alone in their journey
  • Prayer and encouragement are freely given
  • Faith becomes a shared experience

Why Lifted?

In a world that often feels isolating, many of us carry burdens we can't share openly. Lifted was created to change that.

100% Anonymous - No registration, no emails, no personal data collected. Just pure, authentic prayer and confession.

What We Offer

Prayer Requests

Share what you need prayer for. Whether it's health challenges, family struggles, work concerns, or spiritual growth, the community is here to lift you up.

Confessions

Carry burdens alone no longer. Share what's weighing on your heart in complete anonymity. Sometimes just naming something helps us heal.

Community Support

When you see someone else's prayer or confession, you can respond with encouragement. Every "Prayed for you" counts. Every message of support matters.

Our Values

  • Anonymity: Your identity is completely protected
  • Respect: We honor all beliefs and backgrounds
  • Safety: We have community guidelines to keep the space sacred
  • Faith: We believe in the power of prayer and human connection
  • Honesty: Be yourself without fear of judgment

Community Guidelines

  • Be respectful and kind
  • No hate speech, threats, or explicit content
  • No spam or scams
  • Support others authentically
  • Remember you're speaking to real people
