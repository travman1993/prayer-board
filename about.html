About Lifted

Our Mission

Lifted is an anonymous online community dedicated to providing a safe, judgment-free space for people to share their deepest prayers and confessions. We believe that every person deserves to be heard, supported, and uplifted—without fear of judgment or exposure.

Why Anonymous?

Anonymity removes barriers to honest communication. When your identity is protected, you can share what's truly on your heart without worrying about social consequences, reputation, or judgment. This creates a space where vulnerability becomes strength.

Our Values

  • Privacy: We don't collect personal data. Your posts are truly anonymous.
  • Respect: Our community thrives on kindness and compassion for all.
  • Safety: Community-moderated content ensures a supportive environment.
  • Honesty: We believe in the power of truth-telling and authentic expression.
  • Support: Whether through encouragement or listening, we're here for each other.

How It Works

Share what's on your heart—a prayer request or a confession. When you post, you'll receive a supportive response from our community. Read others' prayers and confessions. Show support with a "prayed for you" tap. Help keep the community positive by reporting inappropriate content.

Our Community Commitment

Lifted operates on the principle of community care. Our simple house rules ensure everyone feels safe and respected. Posts are designed to be anonymous and private—they don't persist or get stored on servers.

Questions?

Have questions about how Lifted works? Want to share feedback? Contact us. We'd love to hear from you.
