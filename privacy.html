Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

1. Introduction

Lifted ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website and services.

2. We Do Not Collect Personal Data

This is core to who we are. Lifted is designed to be completely anonymous. We do NOT:

  • Require registration or account creation
  • Collect email addresses
  • Collect phone numbers
  • Collect names or identifiable information
  • Track your identity or location
  • Store IP addresses to identify you
  • Use cookies to track individual users
  • Sell or share user data

3. What We Store

All data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage. This means:

  • Your posts are stored on YOUR device only
  • No server or database stores your personal information
  • Other users see posts in a shared feed, but cannot identify you
  • If you clear your browser cache, your data is deleted

4. Posts and Confessions

When you submit a post:

  • It is saved to your device's local storage
  • It appears in the shared feed (visible to other visitors)
  • No identifying information is attached to it
  • Other users cannot contact or trace you
  • Posts automatically expire after 7 days

5. Third-Party Services

Lifted may display advertisements from third-party ad networks (e.g., Google AdSense). These services may use cookies and tracking technology in accordance with their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for their practices.

6. Browser Data

We may use standard browser data like:

  • User-Agent headers (to optimize display)
  • Referrer information (to understand traffic sources)

This data is not used to identify you personally.

7. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device, there is minimal risk of data breaches on our servers. However, your device's security is your responsibility. We recommend:

  • Using a secure, updated browser
  • Keeping your device software up to date
  • Using strong passwords for your device accounts

8. Children's Privacy

Lifted is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13. If we learn that a child under 13 has submitted personal information, we will delete it.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of material changes by updating the "Last Updated" date on this page. Your continued use of Lifted constitutes acceptance of our updated policies.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about our privacy practices, please contact us.

11. Compliance

Lifted is designed to comply with:

  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation): Since we collect no personal data, GDPR requirements are minimized.
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act): We do not sell personal information, so CCPA obligations are not applicable.
  • Other Privacy Laws: We comply with applicable privacy laws in jurisdictions where we operate.
