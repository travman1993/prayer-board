Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Our Privacy Commitment

Your privacy is important to us. Lifted is completely anonymous—we don't collect personal information, and we don't track individual users.

What We DON'T Collect

  • Email addresses or names
  • IP addresses or device identifiers
  • Location data
  • Demographic information

What We DO Collect

When you post to Lifted, we store only:

  • Your text content (anonymously)
  • Interaction counts (prayed, reported)

This information is stored temporarily in your browser only and does not persist across page refreshes.

Data Security

All data is stored locally in your browser. We do not send your posts to any server or external service. Your content is yours alone.

Third-Party Services

Lifted does not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or data collection services.

Cookies

We use one local storage item (lifted-onboarded) to remember if you've seen our onboarding message. This is stored only on your device.

Children's Privacy

Lifted is not intended for users under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

Your Rights

Your posts are stored only in your browser. You can clear your browser data at any time to delete all posts.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top shows when this policy was last revised.

Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
