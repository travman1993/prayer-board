What We'd Like to Hear

  • Questions: Any question about how Lifted works

Reporting Abuse

If you see a post or comment that violates our community guidelines, you can:

  • Use the Report button: Click "Report" on any post or comment in Lifted
  • Email us: Send details to support@lifted-app.com

Our community team reviews all reports and takes action to keep Lifted a safe space.

Response Time

We aim to respond to all inquiries within 48 hours. However, response times may vary depending on the volume of messages and the complexity of your question.

Community Guidelines Violations

For serious violations of our Terms of Service or community guidelines, you can:

  • Use the Report button on the post
  • Email us with specific details and screenshots if possible

Staying Safe on Lifted

Remember:

  • Lifted is anonymous - don't share personal information
  • Be kind and respectful to others
  • If something feels unsafe, report it
  • Posts are visible to everyone - assume they're public

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I delete my posts?

Posts are stored on your device. To delete them, clear your browser's local storage. Visit our Cookie Policy for instructions.

How long do posts stay on Lifted?

Posts automatically disappear from the feed after 7 days. This helps keep the community fresh and protects privacy.

What if I post by mistake?

Clear your browser's localStorage to remove your posts. See our Cookie Policy for step-by-step instructions.

Is my data safe?

Yes! Lifted stores all data locally on YOUR device. We don't have servers storing your personal information. Read our Privacy Policy for details.

Can you identify me from my post?

No. Lifted is completely anonymous. We don't collect names, emails, or any identifying information.
